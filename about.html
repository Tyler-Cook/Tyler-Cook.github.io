Hello, I am Tyler and am a DMV based IT Administrator for
National Technology Integrators.

A bit about me... I atteneded the Fulton School of Engineering at Arizona State University from 2020-2023 studying full stack web development.


I joined National Technology Integrators (NTI) in 2022 as an Operational Assistant at the DMV based AV integrator. Quickly taking on additional responsibilities, I transitioned into an internal IT support role. By the end of my first year, I successfully implemented Microsoft Intune MDM and Microsoft Defender across our organization. Recognizing my contributions, I was promoted to Systems Administrator. As I enter my third year, I am proud of implementing numerous systems and procedures that significantly enhanced office staff support requests, streamlined onboarding processes, and facilitated the integration of our fourth sister company post-acquisition. It has been a privilege to train and welcome the new team into the NTI family, and I look forward to continuing to contribute to our growth and success.
