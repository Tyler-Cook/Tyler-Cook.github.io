Will Power

Will Power is a fast-paced 2D micro dungeon crawler built in Unity 6 for the 48 hour Kenney Game Jam. The project theme was "POWER". I decided to interpret the theme through the lens of will power, creating a game where the player (Will Power lol) must harness their inner strength to overcome challenging dungeons and enemies to ultimetly pass his squires exam.
